Cornering the Competition

How Jollibee and McDonald's Keep Opening Side by Side in Metro Manila

By Jon Viktor Cabuenas

At the Venice Grand Canal Mall in Taguig, customers deciding between Jollibee and McDonald's — the Philippines' biggest fast food rivals — don't have to walk far. The two restaurants are just 10.7 meters apart.

This is not an isolated case.

Despite competing for many of the same customers, the two repeatedly choose the same commercial areas. Data from the Google Maps Places API shows that Metro Manila's two most ubiquitous fast-food chains frequently become neighbors.

The full picture

Together, the two chains operate hundreds of stores across Metro Manila. At first glance, the dense network of stores makes it difficult to tell whether neighboring locations are simply a product of urban density or evidence of a broader pattern.

The pattern emerges

Filtering the map to show only branches within 250 meters of each other begins to reveal a pattern. Neighboring stores appear across major commercial districts, suggesting that the two frequently occupy the same high-demand areas.

Closer than expected

Narrowing the distance to 100 meters removes several pairs, but the trend remains. At this range, many neighboring branches occupy the same intersection, shopping center, or commercial strip.

Sharing the same space

At 50 meters, the remaining pairs are often in the same building or adjacent developments. At this proximity, customers can easily choose between the two restaurants.

Nearly adjacent

Only six pairs remain. These stores are separated by little more than a storefront or walkway.

The closest competitors

At Venice Grand Canal Mall in Taguig, the two rivals are separated by only 10.7 meters.

The closest competitors

That's close enough that diners can move from one shop to another in seconds!

In Metro Manila, competition between Jollibee and McDonald's is not just about menus or marketing. It's also about location — and sometimes, cornering the competition means opening right next door.
